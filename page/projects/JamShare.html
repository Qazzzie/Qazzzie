Jam Share solves the problem that Musicians encounter when they want to play and mix instrument audio together remotely and live by streaming MIDI or microphone player audio one at a time, which is mixed together and looped subsequently. Jam Share does this in the modern browser Firefox by streaming sound to AntMedia Server which streams to all other clients in the session, starting with the drummer for example to lie down the beat. Then the bassist can follow along with that track and record themselves next while listening to playback of the drummer audio (recommended with earphones). This can then be mixed together and saved.
